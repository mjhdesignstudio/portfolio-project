Messing around with music since the 80's


While I started playing my first instrument Viola, in fifth grade, as you can see in the photo above, I have been around music since I was very little. As a young child, I remember almost always having some type of music playing around the house. Whether it was classical or country, music was a constant in my life.
I played for the local youth symphony from middle school through high school and was fortunate enough to travel with them to Italy and later Germany with my university's symphony orchestra. Since then, I have started playing several other instruments including violin, acoustic guitar, bass guitar, and even some piano.
